Who We Are

Ghar Ka Zaika

"Every dish that leaves our kitchen carries the same love we cook for our own family."

ARoma Fusion is a home-based kitchen in Glass Factory Chowk, Rawalpindi. We started with one simple belief — that the best food is always home-cooked food. No shortcuts, no frozen ingredients, no commercial compromise.

Our menu is rooted in traditional Pakistani recipes — Chicken Achari, Desi Sabzis, slow-cooked Qeema Karaly, and whole wheat Parathas that taste just like amma's kitchen.
